# 💻 Gelan Cyber-Boys

> *A student-driven tech subclub from Gelan Boys' Boarding School — building, learning, and sharing innovation.*

---

##  About Us
**Gelan Cyber-Boys** is a technology-focused subclub where students explore the world of computing, software development, cybersecurity, and digital creativity.

The club serves as a **collaborative platform** where members:
- Learn modern tech skills
- Build real-world projects
- Share knowledge with peers
- Publish and showcase their work

---

## 🎯 Mission
To empower students with **practical technology skills** and foster a culture of **innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning**.

---

## 🧠 What We Do
- 💡 Develop software projects (web, apps, tools)
- 🔐 Explore cybersecurity and ethical hacking basics
- 🌐 Learn web development (frontend & backend)
- 🤖 Experiment with AI and automation
- 🛠️ Solve real-world problems through tech

---

##  Student Projects
This organization is a **hub for student-created work** (may or moy not add some from teachers as well), including:
- Personal and team projects
- Open-source contributions
- Experiments and prototypes
- Learning repositories

> Every member is encouraged to publish and maintain their own projects here.

##  Tech Stack
We work with a variety of tools and technologies, including:

- **Languages:** Python, JavaScript, C/C++, Java  
- **Web:** HTML, CSS, React, Node.js  
- **Tools:** Git, GitHub, Firebase  
- **Other:** Linux, Networking basics, AI APIs
